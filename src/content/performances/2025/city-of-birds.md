*allerdings* presented an improvisation as part of a concert titled <i>Herbert Lauermann, zum 70. Geburstag</i>. He is an Austrian composer who taught for many years at the University of Music and Performing Arts in Vienna (mdw). Lisa Helmel studied with us from 2019 to 2024 at the mdw where we all had classes with Prof. Lauermann.
