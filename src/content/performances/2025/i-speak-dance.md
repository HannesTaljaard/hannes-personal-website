<b>AMBO</b> collaborated as ´sounders´ and as ´movers´ during this performance: we improvised sounds to accompany dancers, performed an improvisation on percussion instruments, and participated as part of the ensemble of movement artists. More information can be found on the website of the <a href="https://www.holistic-dance.at/" target="_blank">holistic dance institute</a>.
